Features

Browse. Discover. Extend.

A clean anime catalogue app powered by AniList, with community extensions for extra functionality.

01

Anime Catalogue

Paginated grid of anime from the AniList GraphQL API. Filter by genre and format, or search by title.

02

Extensions

Install community-built extension mods to add sources and features. Browse and manage them from the Extensions screen.

03

Favourites

Save anime to a local list. No account, no sync, no cloud — your data stays on your device.

04

Discover

Mood-based recommendations, a random anime picker, and an anime wallpaper browser for when you don't know what to watch.

05

Anime Detail

Title, score, genres, synopsis, episode count, cover art, and AniList enrichment data — all in one screen.

06

Settings

Light, dark, or system theme with a custom accent color palette. Language switching and per-source toggles.
